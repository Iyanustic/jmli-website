About Us

The Juvenile Mentorship Leadership Initiative (JMLI) is a non-profit organization dedicated to raising the next generation of value-driven leaders. We are committed to equipping young people with the right mindset, knowledge, and opportunities that enable them to thrive and impact their communities positively.

Our Vision

To save the future generals through the word of God from the ravaging storms and wind of godlessness in our generation. To restore hope and put smile on the faces of millions of childten and teenagers who underpriviledged.

Our Mission

To mentor, guide, and empower young people under 30 years old through structured programs, leadership training, and community-based initiatives. We reach beneficiaries through outreaches in villages, orphanages, churches, schools, streets, and highways, fostering both personal and societal growth.

Our Core Values

Integrity

Building a culture of honesty and accountability.

Excellence

Striving for quality and diligence in all we do.

Leadership

Inspiring influence, responsibility, and positive change.

Service

Impacting lives through dedication and compassion.
