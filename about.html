About JMLI

The Juvenile Mentorship Leadership Initiative (JMLI) is a non-profit organization dedicated to raising, mentoring, and empowering young people to become responsible and impactful leaders in their communities and beyond.

We believe that every young person has the potential to achieve greatness when given the right guidance, mentorship, and opportunities. Through structured programs, leadership training, counselling, and resources, JMLI nurtures the next generation to lead with integrity, vision, and compassion.

Our Mission

To provide mentorship, leadership training, and personal development opportunities that prepare young people to face the challenges of today while shaping a brighter future.

Our Vision

To see a generation of empowered, confident, and visionary leaders who impact their communities positively and drive global change.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Leadership
  • Mentorship
  • Service
  • Excellence
